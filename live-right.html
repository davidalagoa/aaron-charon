Live Right® is a Nigerian-based healthy food delivery service that makes delicious wholesome meals.

Live Right’s tagline, “Eat to live, live to eat” makes the connection between proper nutrition and longevity. Healthy dining is promoted not just as a necessity but also a stylish lifestyle that is a part of your daily activities.
