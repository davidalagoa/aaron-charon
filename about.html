about

I am David Alagoa, a self-taught UI/UX and Graphic Designer. Initially, the plan was to be a music producer, writer, comic illustrator, electrical and electronics engineer but I found myself designing so much that I began to charge for my services.

I believe that creativity is insanity that begins to make sense and bringing ideas and dreams to reality is our primary goal in life.

my process

first principles

I love to dive into the most basic details of any project I work on. Knowing what exactly it is, why it is meant to be that way and how to approach it properly is the first part of my process. This helps me understand what people need and have a solid foundation to build on.

punch in,
punch out

My design workflow is mostly a highlight reel of intense research, experimentation, sweat, tears, and reflection. I make sure everything is put under stress-tests, that way I know what I need to fix or throw into my box of “ok-that-didn’t-work” ideas

sprezzatura

“A certain nonchalance, so as to conceal all art and make whatever one does or says appear to be without effort and almost without any thought about it”

- Baldassare Castiglione's The Book of the Courtier

Basically, think ballerina performing her solo. That’s how I love my projects to feel.
