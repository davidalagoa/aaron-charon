eu migration policy

background

I was commissioned by the correspondent, an online news publication based in the Netherlands to design illustrations for their feature story on the vague nature of migration policies within the EU as related to Africa.

This was an interesting assignment as I got to visualise and represent the concept of borders, immigration based on employment and refugee status, and the vague nature of the policies put down by the EU to reduce the in-flux of immigrants into Europe
