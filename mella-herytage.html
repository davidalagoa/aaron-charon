background

A friend of mine asked me to help her design something that shows the diversity of languages in Africa. She was working on a brand that promotes African Unity in the diaspora and I found it interesting that there is a general assumption that each country on the continent speaks one language. So we came up with this idea of a mosaic showing different languages on the map of Africa

This was a fun project for me because I learnt a lot about the various languages and tribes in different parts of Africa and including my home country, Nigeria. Here are some of the designs I came up with.

illustrations

After this version, we experimented with adding more languages into the design. We discovered it came across as nice but the text within wasn't legible.

Because we didn't want the designs to be monotone (personally, I liked the monotone designs better), she suggested adding some colors to the text to give it a lively appearance.
